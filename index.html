At Mom’s Star, we understand that every child with Autism and Developmental Delay has a unique story. Our holistic approach goes beyond traditional therapy sessions. We create personalized developmental roadmaps tailored to each child’s individual needs, abilities, and progress.

Our Services

Speech Therapy

Play-based activities to improve communication skills, speech sounds, language comprehension, and social interaction.

Occupational Therapy

Helps children gain independence in daily activities, fine/gross motor skills, sensory processing, and social skills.

ABA Therapy

Applied Behavior Analysis therapy provides structured interventions to encourage positive behaviors and learning.
